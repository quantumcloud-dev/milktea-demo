About Us

Barangay 121 Zone 12 is situated at the long street and the famous street of Tolentino. Barangay 121 has a well-known street, namely Facundo that connects Tolentino and Aurora extension, most of the constituents is residing in 618 Tolentino Street and Tolentino interior.

Barangay 121 is very accessible since this area is open for transportations, we have Toda, it is also open for Taxis and more importantly, it is along jeepney line. Our constituents can easily go to public market, supermarket and nearby malls, they can walk going to those sadid establishments. Our barangay had several chairmen that lead in the past, before our Chairman, Cesar Gatdula Jr, his father lead this barangay for a long period of time

This is where he learned his leadership and decided to follow his father footstep Cesar Gatdula Sr., and continue what his father has started. As of now the establishments in the area of our barangay includes Medical Clinic, Minimarts, Sari-sari Store and the biggest one is Liwaywat Marketing Corporation. Liwayway Marketing Corporation formerly known as Liwayway Gawgaw is also the marker of the famous and all time favorite Oishi snacks. Barangay 121 Zone 12 officials and staff are doing our best to give extra mile to serve our constituents.
